# Weekly Review

Append-only journal of what happened each week. One section per week, newest at the top. Spend ~15 minutes on this on Friday or Saturday — the discipline is more valuable than the polish.

The aim is not to write a market commentary. The aim is to surface patterns: setups you're missing, mistakes you're repeating, market conditions that aren't matching your strategies.
